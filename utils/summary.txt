HPL Electric & Power Limited has released its annual report for FY 2023-24 and announced its 32nd Annual General Meeting (AGM).  The AGM will be held virtually on September 30, 2024, at 11:30 AM IST.  The record date for dividend eligibility is September 23, 2024, and the book closure period is from September 24-30, 2024.  E-voting will be available from September 27-29, 2024.  The annual report has been sent to all members with registered email addresses.  The notice provides further details on registration and voting procedures.
